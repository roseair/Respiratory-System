Space Facts

  1. Space does not begin at a specific altitude above the Earth, but the Kármán line at 100 km is a commonly used definition.
  2. There are an estimated 100-400 billion stars in our galaxy, the Milky Way.
  3. In the observable universe there are an estimated 2 trillion (2,000,000,000,000) galaxies.
  4. Spacecraft have visited all the known planets in our solar system.
  5. The space between galaxies is not completely empty but has an average of one atom per cubic meter.
